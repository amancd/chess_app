# Chess Tips & Tricks - Support & Information

## About the App

Chess Tips & Tricks is a comprehensive chess application designed for both beginners and advanced players. With powerful AI opponents, historic games, opening databases, and advanced game analysis, this app is your ultimate chess companion.

---

## Features

### Play Chess Your Way
- **Offline Chess Mode** - Play with a friend on the same device
- **AI Opponents** - Challenge AI bots of different skill levels:
  - Beginner
  - Intermediate
  - Master
  - Grandmaster

### Learn & Explore Chess History
- **Gambit Database** - Study aggressive openings and tactical traps
- **Historic Chess Games** - Replay legendary matches from world champions
- **Complete Chess Openings Guide** - Explore all major chess openings with categorized variations

### Advanced Game Analysis
- **Game Review Mode** - Analyze your games, detect blunders, mistakes, and best moves
- **Move Accuracy & Insights** - Get real-time feedback on your performance

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the app free?**  
A: Yes, the app is free to download and use.

**Q: Can I play offline?**  
A: Yes, you can play against AI opponents or in same-device mode without an internet connection.

**Q: What chess opening systems are included?**  
A: The app includes a comprehensive database of major chess openings, gambits, and defensive systems with ECO codes.

**Q: How strong are the AI opponents?**  
A: We offer multiple difficulty levels from Beginner to Grandmaster, suitable for players of all skill levels.

**Q: Will my games be saved?**  
A: Game history and cloud sync features are planned for future updates.
